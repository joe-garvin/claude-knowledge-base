01 New Discoveries ×4
New
MM/KM
Velours
Kimochi SoundDeep HouseApril 2026

Nearly 15 years since their landmark TTT debut, Mix Mup and Kassem Mosse resurface for Chicago’s Kimochi Sound label. Boomkat describes it as a heads-down, shadowy deep house record in the mode of Theo, Moufang, Actress — with strange integers filling the gaps. Neither artist appears in your library or playlists under this alias. The TTT record was a touchstone for anyone working in this orbit; the follow-up is worth the same attention.

New
Beppu
Objects
Lempuyang / Basic ChannelDub Techno · Abstract DubApril 2026

Andrew Hargreaves returns to a long-dormant alias for this 2LP of abstract dubs on a Basic Channel sublabel, described as nodding toward Pole, Deadbeat, and Pendle Coven. The Lancs dub lineage, the label pedigree, and the Demdike Stare remix connection make this directly on-profile. Not in your library or playlists.

02 New Releases from Artists You Know ×1
In Library
Squarepusher
Kammerkonzert
Warp RecordsOrchestral · IDM · JungleApril 2026

Squarepusher is in your local library (NTS mix). This is a full orchestral LP — drum programming secondary to angular strings and dense chord-change structures, described as 30 years of jazz-fused jungle arriving at a composer form. The Warp description (“onyx-hard, hyperfast riffs”) makes it sound like more than a prestige pivot.

04 Also Noted ×5
01Upsammy & Valentina Magaletti — Seismo (PAN) — featured across both Boomkat emails this week. Early Squarepusher/Matmos reference cluster. Upsammy not in collection. High-priority if you want something more rhythmically active.
02Variant — The Setting Sun (Field Records / Basic Channel) — Stephen Hitchell’s 2008 echospace[detroit] debut gets first vinyl press, remastered by Stefan Betke (Pole). Dub techno from the deep end. You don’t have it; probably should.
03Roméo Poirier — Off Off The Record (Faitiche) — Addendum to his cult Faitiche LP. Jan Jelinek’s label rarely misses. Plunderphonics territory.
04Untold — HEK036 (Hemlock Recordings) — first solo EP in 7 years. Post-dubstep, whirring and sinuous. Hemlock sits adjacent to Hessle Audio.
05Alva Noto — Xerrox series remaster (Noton) — Vols. 1–3 back in remastered form. You have Ryuichi Sakamoto in your playlists; the Alva Noto collaboration records may be a gap worth noting.

Strong week — MM/KM is the headline pick, Beppu is the deep-profile find. Upsammy & Magaletti narrowly missed the main list and belongs on your radar.
