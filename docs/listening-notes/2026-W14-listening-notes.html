02New Releases from Artists You Know ×3
Various Artists / Villalobos
In Library
Various Artists (curated by Ricardo Villalobos)
When There Is No Sun
Omni SoundDeep House · Dub · Cosmic JazzMarch 27, 2026

Villalobos-curated Sun Ra remix comp on a New York/Istanbul independent — 12 tracks reworking the Arkestra's Living Sky and Sun Ra's poetry recordings. Contributors include Underground Resistance, A Guy Called Gerald, Chez Damier, Calibre, and Villalobos himself with two versions of "I Have Forgotten." RA calls it an adventurous triumph. The comp itself is the discovery — Omni Sound is a new label, mastered by Kassian Troyer at Dubplates & Mastering.

03Wildcard ×2
Various Artists
Wildcard
Various Artists
Pop Ambient 2026
KompaktAmbientDecember 2025 · Vinyl in circulation

26th edition of Kompakt's annual ambient series, curated by Wolfgang Voigt. Pass Into Silence (Tetsuo Sakae) back after ~20 years, Dirk Leyers of Closer Musik, Sebastian Mullaert with Hush Forever. Boomkat called it closer to Eno's original template than the rough-edged Ableton dub that's dominated ambient lately — refreshingly unfussy.
