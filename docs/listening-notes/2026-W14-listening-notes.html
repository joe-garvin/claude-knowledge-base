Pop Ambient 2026
KompaktAmbientDecember 2025 (vinyl now)

26th edition of Kompakt's annual ambient series, curated by Wolfgang Voigt. Pass Into Silence (Tetsuo Sakae) back after ~20 years, Dirk Leyers of Closer Musik, Sebastian Mullaert with Hush Forever.
