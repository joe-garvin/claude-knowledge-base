Music From MemoryShoegaze · Dub · Electro · Trip-hop2026 reissue of 1991 recordings

1991 recordings by A.R. Kane's Rudy Tambala and Cranes vocalist Alison Shaw, finally reissued by Music From Memory with previously unreleased material. Boomkat calls it the most essential Music From Memory release in a hot minute — shoegaze, dub, electro, and trip-hop in that early-'90s pre-Seefeel zone. The A.R. Three years on from the first Borderland session, the two architects of Detroit and Berlin techno reconvene on Tresor for seven expansive techno dub pieces. Japanese enigma Meitei reworks his cult Kofū trilogy with augmented results shaped by touring environments. Not in the collection. Dal Forno's fourth album was recorded in an abandoned hospital — rattly, dub-inflected vignettes about a friendship turned romance.
