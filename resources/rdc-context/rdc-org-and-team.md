## Important distinctions

**In-house creative team** (under Naj): Produces deliverables for clients from RDC's own operations. This is the core Creative Team — content writers, visual designers, motion graphics, video, and project managers.

**Embedded / integrated team** (under Amy Metzler): Works inside Microsoft's environment day-to-day. These are RDC professionals but their context, tools, and constraints are those of a large enterprise. They are not part of the in-house Creative Team workflow.

**VSS account team** (under Erin Genest): Client-facing team managing the Visual Studio Subscriptions relationship. Includes program managers and at least one visual designer.

**Former key staff:** Kristen Baker (Sr. Director of Growth & Marketing) departed early 2026. She was a significant organizational figure responsible for business strategy and client growth; her absence may be relevant context for understanding current leadership structure.
