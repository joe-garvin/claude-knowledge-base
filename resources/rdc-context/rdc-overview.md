# RDC overview

Red Door Collaborative (RDC) is a minority-owned marketing and consulting firm founded in 2010, headquartered in Bellevue, WA. It is certified as a Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) through the NMSDC and is also recognized as a Woman-Owned Business Enterprise (WMBE). The company is led by Managing Partners Chris Telfer (Creative Director) and Jenny Telfer (Social Media Strategy).

RDC occupies a hybrid position in the market: it combines the strategic depth of a consultancy with the execution agility of a creative studio. Its model spans three distinct service modes — in-house creative production, project-based consulting, and embedded talent/staff augmentation inside client organizations.

## Mission and vision

**Mission:** Empower brands with strategic, creative, and data-driven marketing solutions. Through a collaborative approach, deliver personalized strategies that drive growth, enhance market presence, and create lasting impact.

**External vision:** A world where creativity and strategy seamlessly unite to empower businesses with innovative solutions. Transform brands by crafting meaningful, authentic experiences that connect with audiences and drive lasting impact.

**Social media mission:** Foster connection and collaboration at the intersection of human intelligence and artificial intelligence. Share work, behind-the-scenes conversations, and honest insights that demonstrate how creativity, empathy, and emerging technology combine to create meaningful impact. Prioritize clarity over clicks, curiosity over certainty, and collaboration over self-promotion.

## Positioning

RDC positions itself as a strategic, creative, and scalable partner for mid-to-large enterprises and tech-focused companies. The tagline is: **Strategic. Creative. Scalable.**

**Positioning statement:** For mid-to-large enterprises and tech-focused companies who need strategic branding, creative solutions, consulting, and impactful storytelling, Red Door Collaborative is a specialized marketing agency that provides personalized, high-quality consulting, strategic marketing, and creative services. It offers a customized approach that prioritizes collaboration, agility, and delivering measurable results through a diverse and experienced team.

**Primary external message:** Red Door Collaborative is your trusted partner for personalized, creative, and measurable marketing solutions that connect with your audience and drive business growth.

## Value proposition

RDC collaborates with businesses to translate their complex needs into innovative, engaging, and data-driven marketing solutions that drive growth, foster deeper customer relationships, and elevate brand identity.

## Brand pillars

**Collaborative** — Functions as an extension of the client's internal teams. Agile, customized, and communication-forward. Open processes ensure seamless integration.

**Scalable** — Provides embedded consultants, marketers, and marketing services to fill talent gaps, scale teams, and accelerate client initiatives. Whether addressing resource shortages or specific expertise needs, experienced professionals integrate quickly to drive results.

**Creative** — Crafts innovative solutions that capture attention, tell powerful stories, and bring brands to life. Specializes in content creation, branding, video production, and digital experiences.

## Differentiators

- **Integrated expertise:** Bridges creative execution and strategic planning; single point of contact for scalable brand and marketing initiatives.
- **Flexible engagement models:** Both project-based delivery and embedded team models, from high-touch consulting to ongoing creative production.
- **Enterprise-level experience:** Team has led initiatives for Fortune 500 tech companies and fast-scaling innovators alike.
- **Certified and diverse:** NMSDC-certified MBE; dedicated to DEI in work and partnerships. Brings naturally inclusive lens to all projects.

## Tone of voice

Professional, humble, approachable, collaborative, creative, down to earth, fun, smart and sharp, witty, kind.

In practice (especially in social/external content): grounded rather than grandiose; conversational rather than corporate; curious rather than prescriptive; inclusive rather than exclusive; authentic rather than performative. Think: "trusted colleague over coffee," not broadcast channel.

## Target client profile

Mid-to-large enterprises and technology companies. Specifically: enterprise software providers, big data and AI companies, SaaS companies, high-value tech startups. Senior marketing leaders (CMOs, VPs), CEOs, decision-makers, and tech entrepreneurs.

**Client pain points RDC addresses:**
- Lack of resources or expertise to create content at scale
- Inability to do technical and data-driven marketing for complex tech stacks
- Headcount allocation constraints
- Difficulty communicating clear, concise, compelling value props
- Struggle to generate awareness and engagement
- Difficulty with customer retention or expansion
- Budget constraints and ROI pressure
- Difficulty managing and providing detailed guidance on projects
