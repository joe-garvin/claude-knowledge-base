# RDC services and clients

## Service model overview

RDC operates across three engagement modes:

**In-house creative production** — The core Creative Team produces deliverables for clients: content writing, visual design, motion graphics, video, project management. This is the majority of day-to-day work.

**Project-based consulting** — RDC plans and executes discrete client projects, develops milestones and schedules, and ensures outcomes are delivered on time.

**Embedded consulting / staff augmentation** — RDC sources, hires, trains, and onboards talent that works inside client organizations as an extension of their teams. This is a major differentiator and growth area. Embedded consultants become part of the client's internal workflow.

**Managed services** — End-to-end solutions in which RDC staffs teams of specialists to operate an ongoing, repeatable business function or process for the client.
