# Length and pacing

Targets for every section and speaker type. Use these to calibrate outlines and scripts.

---

## Overall

- **Final episode:** 5–6 minutes on camera
- **Script length:** 6–8 pages when formatted with speaker tags and bullets
- **Outline length:** May approach script length in word count — that is expected and correct

---

## Per section

| Section | Target |
|---|---|
| Social copy | 5–6 bullets, ~100–120 words total |
| Introduction | 3–4 exchanges, very brief — roughly half a page |
| Each body section | 2–4 exchanges; ~1–1.5 pages of script per topic |
| Demo segment | Variable; keep narration tight |
| CTA / next steps | 1–2 exchanges |
| Outro | ~half a page |

---

## Per speaker turn

| Speaker | Bullets | Word count |
|---|---|---|
| Thomas (question) | 1–2 bullets | 20–50 words |
| Thomas (bridge/validation) | 1–2 bullets | 15–30 words |
| Guest (answer) | 3–5 bullets | 100–250 words |
