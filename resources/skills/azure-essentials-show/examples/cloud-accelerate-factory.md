I. Social promo section [Thomas]

f. Don't let cloud complexity slow you down—watch this episode and discover how to get expert help with a wide variety of Azure scenarios, including migration, analytics and AI!

II. Cloud Accelerate Factory overview and benefits [Ravi]
a. Overview
   i. Cloud Accelerate Factory is a benefit of Azure Accelerate, built from the patterns and insights we've gathered from thousands of customer deployments.
   ii. The Factory gives you zero-cost deployment assistance for over 30 Azure services, with hands-on Microsoft support that's grounded in proven best practices and available for projects of any size, anywhere in the world.
   iii. Whether you're refactoring, rehosting, or replatforming workloads--or building a new AI-enabled app--you can take advantage of Factory assistance in coordination with your team and Azure partner – at no additional cost.
   iv. Cloud Accelerate Factory is built on a joint delivery model between your Azure partner and Microsoft to make your delivery experience even better. Factory experts handle the predictable, repeatable delivery tasks, so your partner can focus on what they do best: solving complex challenges, delivering custom solutions, and guiding your long-term cloud strategy.
b. Key benefits [visual aid: slide]
   i. You get zero-cost assistance from Microsoft to deploy more than 30 Azure services through joint delivery with an Azure partner, no matter how big or small your project is.
   ii. You can accelerate your deployments and complete them in just weeks by leveraging proven strategies we've developed over thousands of customer engagements.
   iii. You can maximize your investments by prioritizing your funding and skilled resources for the more advanced components or projects that will boost your business impact.

III. Cloud Accelerate Factory scope [Ravi]
a. Cloud Accelerate Factory provides you with hands-on support for refactoring, replatforming and bringing your workloads to Azure. The Factory focuses on technical delivery tasks to help you or your preferred Azure partner scale faster, while your teams focus on planning, integrations, or advanced workload design.
b. Factory-supported scenarios [visual aid: slide]
   i. The Factory supports a wide range of migration scenarios, from migrating Windows, Linux, and SQL servers to modernizing databases and apps to Azure PaaS services.
   ii. It also offers help with specialized scenarios like implementing Azure Virtual Desktop, enabling security services like Defender and Sentinel, and deploying analytics solutions.
   iii. Plus, Cloud Accelerate Factory supports emerging areas like developing GenAI assistants with Azure OpenAI and migrating to Azure VMware Solution.

IV. Cloud Accelerate Factory end-to-end process [Ravi]
a. Think of this as a guided sprint model — waves of applications or services in 3 to 6-week delivery cycles.
b. Once you're nominated and approved, our Factory team quickly assess your environment, align on prerequisites, and then gets to work — all in partnership with your Microsoft team or Azure partner of choice.
c. The Cloud Accelerate Factory follows a four-step, end-to-end process [visual aid: slide]
   i. First, we handle project prerequisites by conducting a Factory Assessment and validating or building your Landing Zone.
   ii. Next, we finalize the scope based on the assessment output and get your approval while outlining your migration journey and finalizing the project execution method.
   iii. Then comes project execution, where once you approve, we execute the project using our established factory model.
   iv. Finally, we validate everything by performing workload validation and getting your final signoff.

V. How to get started/CTA [Ravi]
a. How to get started [visual aid: slide]
   i. Engaging with our Factory team is simple. Once you've defined your scope, your Microsoft account team can nominate you for Factory assistance. Once your nomination is accepted, our Factory experts will start by identifying the right workloads and then align on roles and timing with you and your partner to begin the Phase 1 migration sprint.
b. The next step we want viewers to take
   i. You can learn more on the Azure Accelerate website and work with your Microsoft account team or partner to get started.
