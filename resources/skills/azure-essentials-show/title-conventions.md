# Title conventions

Rules for episode titles. Always produce 2–3 options at the outline stage.

---

## Rules

- 4–6 words
- Start with an action verb
- Customer-focused — speak to the benefit or value to the viewer, not the product name
- For series episodes: use the "Series Name: Specific Episode Focus" pattern

---

## Successful patterns

**Action verb + prep phrase:**
- "Save on Azure SQL with Hybrid Benefit"
- "Build Intelligent Apps on Azure"
- "Accelerate Cloud Adoption with Expert Guidance"

**Series episode:**
- "Azure Cost Estimation: Plan Confidently with the Azure Pricing Calculator"
- "Azure Cost Estimation: Your Strategic Guide to Cloud Pricing"

---

## What to avoid

- Titles that lead with the product name rather than the benefit
- Generic verbs with no specificity ("Learn About," "Explore")
- Titles longer than 6 words
- Working titles from kickoff materials — these almost always need refinement
