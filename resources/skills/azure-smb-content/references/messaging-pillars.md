# Messaging pillars

## SMB hero statement (short)

"Bring Azure AI to your business: Build and run AI apps and agents on Microsoft Azure that are secure, cost-effective, and adaptable to your needs. Get future-proof technology, with ease and predictability."

## SMB expanded positioning

"Grow your business with Microsoft Azure: Easily create and manage AI apps and agents on a complete platform that adapts to your needs — helping you empower your teams to do more, reduce repetition-induced cost centers, and bring delight to your customers. With built-in security and cost controls, you get the latest AI tools, without needing to expand the IT department."

---

## Four messaging pillars (FY26 H1)

| Pillar | Tagline | SMB angle |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Build AI solutions faster with fewer developers | "Build AI with AI" | Lean teams can do more with GitHub Copilot and AI-assisted tools |
| Orchestrate AI systems on a unified platform | "Orchestrate at ease" | One place to connect models, data, and agents — no manual stitching |
| Scale when ready with confidence | "Scale as needed" | Start small, grow without re-architecting; no dedicated ops team needed |
| Secure, govern, and ensure safety of your AI | "Secure by default" | Built-in protections; no security specialist required |

---

## Problems Azure solves for SMBs (copy-ready framing)

- Limited budgets and IT resources make advanced technology hard to access → affordable, pay-as-you-go pricing
- Security and data protection are constant worries → automatic updates and built-in fraud protection
- Fragmented tools and complex updates slow progress → all-in-one platform, everything works together
- Staying ahead of competitors and keeping customers engaged → proven tools used by businesses worldwide, now tailored for SMBs
