# Social card template

## Two-part structure

1. **Short benefit-led statement (4–7 words)** — outcome or benefit, not process
2. **Supporting line** — expands with specifics or a call to action

## Writing rules

- Keep language direct and declarative
- Lead with aspiration over urgency
- Outcome or benefit in the first line — never lead with a product name or feature
- The supporting line should add something specific, not restate the first line in different words
